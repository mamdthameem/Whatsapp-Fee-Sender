# Digital Fee Receipt Portal — SSEC

A simple web tool that lets college staff send fee receipts to students on WhatsApp — no printing, no manual distribution, no technical skills needed.

---

## What It Does

Staff open the website, upload a student's fee receipt PDF, type their WhatsApp number, and click Send. The student receives the PDF on WhatsApp within seconds.

---

## How to Use

1. Open the website: [wa-pdf.web.app](https://wa-pdf.web.app)
2. Enter the student's WhatsApp number (10 digits — the +91 is already filled in)
3. Upload the fee receipt PDF (drag and drop or click to browse)
4. Click **Send Receipt**
5. A green notification will confirm it was delivered

That's it. No login, no installation, no technical knowledge needed.

---

## Who Can Use This

Any staff member at SSEC who handles fee receipts. The portal is only accessible to those who have the link.
